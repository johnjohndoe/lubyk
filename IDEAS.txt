OSC control
===========

Add a node "osc.all" that uses two public methods available in every node:

setParam(url, value)

onNotify(callback)

The callback is:

onNotify(function(url, value)
end)

Publish OSC with _osc._udp

Learn
=====

Click 'learn' param (osc.all). Sent OSC message, notify change = two way mapping.

Editor
======

Raccourci pour aller à la première erreur (depuis dernier lock).
